Concept 2021-02-07 This is a first pass at making the text of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) more accessible and responsive. The UN pages are handsome be require much clicking to navigate through all the goals. The Wikipedia list of the goals requires much scrolling.

In the solution presented here, istead of having to open links to new pages, the data is revealed by single clicks.

The data freshly loaded from Wikipedia every time you use the app. Any changes there are automatically updated viewable here.

Further revisions of this effort will add: prettier CSS styling, links to pages with more details and references, tags to indicate appropriateness for a variety of circumstances such as manufacturing, constriction, healthcare, fitness, communications and more.
