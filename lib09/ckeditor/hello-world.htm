Bilingual 123 Personality Disorder 

This is may be the first time you hear about this made-up disorder but it actually isn’t so far from the truth. As recent studies show, the language you speak has more effects on you than you realize. According to the studies, the language a person speaks affects their cognition, behavior, emotions and hence their personality.
